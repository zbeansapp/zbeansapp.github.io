Coliseum Hospital

The third coffee shop located inside the Coliseum Hospital in downtown Macon symbolizes quick convenience. The shop has a small footprint inside the main entrance of the hospital, but it comes with a plethora of choices. It offers a full service beverage bar, including our signature coffee based drinks and our fresh fruit smoothies. Additionally, the shop has an assortment of fresh pastries and ready-to-go sandwiches.

Within the Coliseum hospital system in Downtown Macon, the coffee shop also offers a delivery system. Here, doctors, nurses, and staff at the hospital may call in, so we can deliver it to their buildings. This delivery system opens up the menu to everyone, ensuring everyone is caffeinated for the next big decision!
